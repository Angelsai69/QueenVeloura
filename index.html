FAQ
Is the hair 100% human hair?
Yes. All Veloura wigs are made from 100% natural virgin human hair — no synthetic blends, ever.
Can I colour and heat-style the hair?
Yes. You can wash, curl, straighten, dye, and style our hair exactly like your natural hair.
How long will the hair last?
With proper care, Veloura wigs last 2–3 years. Our quality guarantee covers the first 90 days.
How do I find my cap size?
Measure around your head at the hairline. Small: under 21.5", Medium: 21.5"–22.5", Large: over 22.5". When in doubt, Medium fits most.
What is HD Lace?
HD (High Definition) lace is ultra-thin and melts into all skin tones for an undetectable hairline. It is more delicate than standard lace and requires gentle handling.
How do I care for my wig?
Wash with sulphate-free shampoo, condition mid-length to ends, air dry on a wig stand, and store in a satin bag when not worn.
How long does shipping take?
US orders: 3–7 business days after 1–3 day processing. International: 7–15 business days.
Can I return my wig?
Yes, if unused and within 7 days of delivery. See our Returns Policy for full details.

Hair Care & Expectations
Last Updated: March 19, 2026  ·  Queen Veloura
We are committed to providing high-quality 100% natural virgin human hair. Because our products are made from natural human hair, each item may have slight variations that make it unique.
1. Natural Hair Characteristics
  • Slight variations in texture, wave pattern, or colour tone may occur
  • Hair may react differently to styling tools, humidity, or products
  • Some minimal shedding is normal for human hair extensions and wigs
These characteristics are natural and are not considered product defects.
2. Product Appearance
Product photos represent the general style and texture of the hair. Due to lighting, monitor settings, and the natural characteristics of human hair, the product you receive may appear slightly different.
3. Before Installation
Before installing, please inspect the hair carefully, verify it matches your order, and ensure you are satisfied with the quality. Once installed, cut, coloured, washed, or altered, the hair cannot be returned.
4. Proper Hair Care
  • Wash with sulphate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Gently detangle with a wide-tooth comb or brush
  • Avoid excessive heat styling — use heat protectant when using hot tools
  • Store wigs properly on a wig stand when not in use
5. Chemical Treatments
Virgin hair can be dyed, bleached, or chemically treated. For best results, have treatments performed by a professional stylist and always perform a strand test first. Damage from improper chemical processing is not considered a product defect.
6. Expected Lifespan
With proper care, high-quality virgin human hair can last several months to over a year, depending on frequency of use, styling methods, and maintenance routines.
7. Return Eligibility
Hair is eligible for return only if unused, uninstalled, and in original packaging with security ties intact. Please refer to our Returns & Refunds policy for full details.

Wig Buyer Guide
Before You Buy — What Every Customer Should Know
1. Human Hair is Natural
Each bundle or wig may have slight differences in texture or wave pattern. Colour tones may vary slightly between batches. The hair behaves like natural hair when styled — these variations are normal and part of the beauty of authentic human hair.
2. Choosing Length
Hair length is measured when fully stretched. Curly or wavy hair may appear shorter than straight hair of the same length. For a fuller look, choose higher density wigs. Contact us before ordering if unsure.
3. Choosing Texture
  • Straight — sleek and versatile
  • Body Wave — soft natural waves
  • Deep Wave — defined, voluminous waves
  • Curly / Kinky Curly — full natural curl pattern
Curly and wavy hair may change slightly depending on styling and humidity.
4. Wig Density
  • 130% — natural everyday look
  • 150% — full and popular choice
  • 180%+ — extra volume and glam styles
5. Lace Wig Expectations
Lace wigs create a natural-looking hairline. You may need to trim the lace, apply adhesive, and customise baby hairs. Many customers have their wigs installed by a professional stylist for best results.
6. Colour Expectations
Most wigs and bundles are natural black or dark brown unless otherwise specified. If you plan to bleach or dye, always use a professional and perform a strand test first.
7. Returns & Hygiene
Wigs are hygiene-sensitive products. We can only accept returns if the hair has not been worn or installed, and remains in original condition with packaging intact. See our Returns Policy for full details.

Hair Care Guide
Proper care will help your Veloura hair remain soft, beautiful, and manageable for months.
1. Washing
  • Gently detangle before washing
  • Use lukewarm water and sulphate-free shampoo
  • Massage gently from top to bottom — never rub or twist
  • Apply moisturising conditioner, leave for a few minutes, then rinse
  • Air dry when possible
2. Detangling
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush
  • Always start from the ends and work up to the roots
  • For curly textures, detangle while slightly damp and conditioned
3. Heat Styling
  • Always apply heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Keep styling tools below 350–400°F when possible
  • Limit excessive heat styling to maintain hair health
4. Moisturising
  • Use light oils or hair serums to maintain shine
  • Apply leave-in conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid heavy oils that can weigh the hair down
5. Nighttime Care
  • Wrap hair with a silk or satin scarf, or use a satin bonnet
  • For long hair, loosely braid or tie into a low ponytail before sleeping
  • A satin pillowcase also works well
6. Storage
  • Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain shape
  • Keep in a clean, dry place away from humidity
7. Chemical Treatments
Our virgin hair can be dyed, bleached, or chemically treated. Always have treatments performed by a professional stylist and perform a strand test first. Over-processing can damage the hair and is not covered under our quality guarantee.
8. Expected Lifespan
With proper care, premium virgin human hair lasts 6 months to over a year depending on frequency of use and styling methods.

Installation Guide
Installing your wig properly creates a natural appearance, secure fit, and long-lasting style.
If you are new to wigs, consider visiting a professional hairstylist for installation.
1. Prepare Your Natural Hair
  • Braid hair into cornrows or create flat braids
  • Wear a wig cap to keep natural hair flat and secure
  • The goal is a smooth, flat base under the wig
2. Apply a Wig Cap
  • Place the wig cap over your head, ensuring all natural hair is tucked under
  • Adjust so it fits comfortably around your hairline
  • Choose a nude or skin-tone cap for the most natural look
3. Trim the Lace
  • Place the wig on your head and mark where your natural hairline falls
  • Carefully trim the lace with small scissors along the hairline
  • Take your time — avoid cutting too much
4. Secure the Wig
Choose the method that suits you:
  • Wig glue or adhesive — longest hold
  • Wig tape — gentle on skin
  • Elastic bands or adjustable straps — glueless method
5. Style the Hairline
  • Pluck the hairline if necessary for a natural density
  • Style baby hairs along the edges
  • Apply foundation or powder along the part to match your scalp tone
6. Style the Wig
After installation, style as desired — straighten, curl, create waves, or wear in updos. Always use heat protectant before hot tools.
7. Wig Removal
  • Gently remove the wig and clean any adhesive residue
  • Store on a wig stand or mannequin head
  • Proper removal protects both your wig and natural hair

Beginner Guide
7 Common Wig Mistakes Beginners Should Avoid
Queen Veloura Journal  ·  Beauty & Wisdom
Wigs are an amazing way to transform your look, but beginners sometimes make simple mistakes that affect how natural the wig appears. Avoiding these will help you achieve better results.
1. Choosing the Wrong Cap Size
A wig that is too tight or too loose can feel uncomfortable and look unnatural. Always measure your head and check the cap size before buying.
2. Skipping Lace Customisation
Many lace wigs require trimming the lace and customising the hairline. Skipping this step can make the wig look less natural.
3. Using Too Much Heat
Human hair wigs can handle heat styling, but excessive heat can dry out the hair and shorten its lifespan. Use moderate temperatures and always apply heat protectant.
4. Neglecting Proper Maintenance
Just like natural hair, wigs require regular care. Washing, conditioning, and detangling your wig will help maintain its beauty.
5. Improper Storage
Throwing your wig into a drawer or bag can cause tangling and damage. Store wigs on a wig stand or mannequin head whenever possible.
6. Overusing Hair Products
Too many heavy oils or styling products can weigh down the hair and cause buildup. Use lightweight products designed for human hair extensions.
7. Installing the Wig Incorrectly
Proper installation is key to achieving a natural appearance. If you are unsure how to install a lace wig, consider watching tutorials or visiting a professional stylist.
Avoiding these simple mistakes will help your wig look more natural and last longer.

Lace vs HD Lace: What's the Real Difference? 🤔
Queen Veloura Journal  ·  Beauty & Wisdom
If you've been shopping for wigs online, you've probably seen two terms everywhere: Lace wigs and HD lace wigs.

But what do they actually mean? And is HD lace really worth it? Let's break it down.
What Is Lace in a Wig?

Lace is the thin material at the front of many wigs that creates the illusion of a natural hairline. It allows the wig to blend with your scalp so it looks like the hair is growing directly from your skin — which is why lace front wigs look so natural when installed correctly.
Standard Lace Wigs

Standard lace is the traditional lace used in many wigs.

Benefits include:
✨ Durable material   ✨ Great for everyday wear   ✨ More affordable

However, regular lace may need a little customisation — such as tinting or makeup — to perfectly match your skin tone.
HD Lace Wigs

HD lace stands for "High Definition" lace. This lace is thinner, softer, and more transparent than traditional lace.

Benefits include:
✨ Extremely natural appearance   ✨ Blends easily with many skin tones   ✨ Less customisation needed

Because HD lace is thinner, the hairline can appear almost invisible when installed properly.
So Which One Should You Choose?

Both options can look beautiful, but the choice depends on your preferences.

Choose standard lace if you want:
✔ Durability   ✔ Affordability   ✔ Everyday wear

Choose HD lace if you want:
✔ Ultra-natural hairline   ✔ Luxury wig look   ✔ Minimal customisation
The Real Secret

No matter which lace you choose, the installation and hair quality make the biggest difference. A well-installed wig with high-quality human hair will always look more natural.

And when everything comes together — the lace, the hairline, and the styling — you get that perfect moment where someone says:

"Wait… that's a wig?!"
